About BookNest Library

BookNest Library is a community-driven space dedicated to promoting literacy, learning, and lifelong education. Our mission is to provide easy access to a wide collection of books, journals, and digital resources for students, professionals, and curious minds of all ages.

Established in 2000, we have grown from a small neighborhood library to a city-wide institution with thousands of members. With modern facilities, computer labs, quiet reading areas, and knowledgeable staff, we aim to create a comfortable environment for everyone to learn and grow.
